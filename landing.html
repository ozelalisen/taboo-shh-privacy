How it works

Learn in 30 seconds,
play for hours

No rulebook needed. Split into teams, grab your phone, and you're ready. Here's all there is to it:

01

Draw a Card

A secret word appears on screen — along with five forbidden clues you absolutely cannot say.

02

Get Creative

Describe, act, hint — do whatever it takes to get your team to guess. Just don't say the forbidden words!

03

Race the Clock

The timer is ticking. Your team shouts answers while the other team watches for slip-ups. Pure adrenaline.

04

Score & Celebrate

Every correct guess earns a point. Build streaks for bonus glory. The team with the most points wins bragging rights!
